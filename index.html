A Taste of Tonga

Try these simple recipes for a taste of Polynesian style cooking!

Lu Pulu

Ingredients

  • Taro Leaves (*Spinach Leaves work as an alternative)
  • Corned Beef
  • Chopped Onions
  • Coconut Milk

Other Supplies

  • Aluminum Foil
  • Knife
  • Spoon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Cut a medium sized square of aluminum foil and lay flat.
  3. Lay taro leaves so that they create a surface large enough to add more ingredients on.
  4. Place a spoonful or two of corned beef on taro leaves.
  5. Sprinkle a spoonful of chopped onions on corned beef.
  6. Pour a spoonful or two of coconut milk on previous ingredients.
  7. Fold corners of aluminum foil up over ingredients and twist so that the contents are sealed without chance of leaking.
